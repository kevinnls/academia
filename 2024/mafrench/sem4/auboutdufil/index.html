«During a period of confinement, a man poses as a Canadian public health official to offer a new Covid-19 testing kit.»

Un court métrage fait «maison», lors du confinement en avril 2020, avec les moyens du bord et avec la petite famille pour aider derrière la caméra. Je remercie également mes précieux comédiens et complices à distance!

To me there is no past or future in art. If a work of art cannot live always in the present it must not be considered at all. The art of the Greeks, of the Egyptians, of the great painters who lived in other times, is not an art of the past; perhaps it is more alive today than it ever was. (Picasso)

Since human nature never changes, the deeper traits of mankind are themselves unaffected by rapidly altering outward conditions. So long as a work of art speaks to these feelings, it will surmount the threatening ravages of time. (Mueller 396)

l’Amour Filial

La classification par un fil réunisseur de style est plus apparente une fois qu’on se dépasse du présent

Contemporary art’s no different: While it might seem like anything goes at art festivals today, give it enough time and art, too, will look distinctly ’90s (Julian Schnabel), ’00s (Julie Mehretu), and ’10s (?). (Kitchener et al.)
